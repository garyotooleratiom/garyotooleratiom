# Gary O'Toole | Ratiom Agency

Gary O'Toole is the founder and Owner-Operator of Ratiom Agency, a website design and digital marketing brand focused on helping local service businesses improve their online credibility, generate more enquiries, and become the easy choice in their market.

Based in Kraków, Poland, Gary has worked in digital marketing since 2004. His experience spans website design, ecommerce, content creation, paid media, conversion rate optimisation, AI automation, and marketing systems.

Over the course of his career, Gary has worked with businesses throughout the United Kingdom and United States, helping improve website performance, customer trust, lead generation, and digital marketing effectiveness.

His background includes ecommerce operations, digital leadership roles, enterprise-level advertising platform support, and more than two decades of practical digital marketing experience.

Today, Gary focuses on building Ratiom Agency and helping local service businesses present themselves more professionally online through:

* Website Design
* Landing Pages
* Content Creation
* Marketing Support
* AI Automation
